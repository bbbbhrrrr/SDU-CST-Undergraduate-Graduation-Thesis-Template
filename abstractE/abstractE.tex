The English abstract should briefly describe the research background, the problem statement, the proposed method, the implementation or evaluation setting, and the final conclusions of the thesis. A length of around 250 content words is usually appropriate. The writing should be concise, accurate, and consistent with the Chinese abstract instead of being a word-for-word translation of the full text.

When the thesis involves system construction, algorithm design, or experimental validation, this section should summarize the technical route and the effectiveness of the proposed solution so that international readers can quickly understand the contribution and practical value of the work.
